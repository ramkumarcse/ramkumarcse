For Project 2 you have 3 possible options. You are required to do only one. You may do more for bonus points.

Project 1

Through simulations obtain the G vs Throughput plots for ALOHA and slotted ALOHA where G is the average number of attempted packet transmissions per packet duration. Instructions

Project 2

Through simulations estimate the optimal value of p for p-persistent CSMA, for G ranging from 1 to 5 where G is the average number of packets queued for transmission per packet duration. Assume all packets have a duration is 1 milli second and that the transmission delay between stations is 1 micro second.

or

Through simulations estimate the optimal parameters for non-persistent CSMA for G ranging from 1 to 5 where G is the average number of packets queued for transmission per packet duration. Assume all packets have a duration is 1 milli second and that the transmission delay between stations is 1 micro second.

or

A channel with a round trip time of 20 ms has a error rate 1 in 1000 packets in each direction. Each packet has a duration of 1 millisecond. Through simulations plot the relationship between throughput and SRP window size
